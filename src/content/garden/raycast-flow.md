Raycast is the only launcher I've stayed with for more than a year. The extensions that earn their slot:

- Clipboard history. The single feature I'd pay the full subscription for.
- Window management. Tiled keyboard shortcuts, no third party app needed.
- Notion quick capture. Friction-free brain dump.
- Snippets. The five lines of boilerplate I'd otherwise paste from the previous PR.

The rest I install and uninstall within a week.
