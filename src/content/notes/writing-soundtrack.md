Music with lyrics breaks my writing. Anything with a hook breaks my writing. So my writing soundtrack is narrow:

- Nils Frahm, Spaces. Piano and texture, nothing demanding attention.
- Ólafur Arnalds, Re:member. Same family, slightly more cinematic.
- Brian Eno, Music for Airports. The original ambient template, still unbeaten.

When I want something with more energy: Hania Rani, Ghosts.
