If a yes isn't a hell yes, it's a no.

I use this as a filter for opportunities, not for daily work. Daily work has too many things you have to do that aren't exciting and shouldn't be. But for the optional things, the side projects, the conference invites, the coffees: hell yes, or pass.

It works because the cost of a soft yes is not the hour you spend. It's the months you carry it as a half-finished thing on your conscience.
